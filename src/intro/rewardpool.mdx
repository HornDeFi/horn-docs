# How am i rewarded when entering a Pool?

<br/>
<hr/>

- On every pool, you get rewarded for participating and being inside it <b>wining horn on a daily basis</b>: the mint system.

- You lock your funds for a chosen pool and a duration, and <b>collect rewards every days</b> from the mint system, you also get rewarded from every new deposit added into that pool after you entered.

- When a new member join a pool, the fixed fees is shared equally through a pool percent shared algorithm and <b>directly sent as rewards</b> to every members already inside the pool.
