# How the daily HORN bonus received works?

<br/>
<hr/>

- By entering and staying actively in a pool you also get <b>rewarded</b> with an <i>Horn</i> bonus, on a <b>daily basis</b> you can claim new Horn token through the pool interface,
  the earned tokens per day depends on every pool, each one offer different rate per day as the risk change from one to another.

- Each pool has a minimal lock time before that you're able to withdraw your pool balance and any extra bonus, giving HORN every day through a safe and decentralized claiming system 
 is a way to <b>reward</b> our members for participating in the protocol and our ecosystem.
