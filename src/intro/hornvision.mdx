# What exactly is Horn and what is our main vision focus?

<br/>
<hr/>

- Horn is a decentralized token <i>ERC-20</i> project, that is willing to offer you a new way of investment and staking rewards with <b>higher possible ROI</b> on the crypto-currency market.

- Basically we want to help to democratize defi, blockchain utilisation for finance utilities, by offering all Horn members a way to invest funds easily, and see it growing by time.
